Our Top Rafting Trips

The Thunder Gorge Challenge (Class IV–V)

  • For adrenaline junkies
  • Heart-pounding rapids
  • Steep drops and narrow canyons
  • Not for the faint-hearted (or dry-hearted)

Lazy River Vibes (Class I–II)

  • Family-friendly float trip
  • Chill currents, lush scenery
  • Great for beginners, kids, and zen seekers
  • Ideal for bonding or sneaky selfies

Sunset Rapids Experience

  • Twilight magic on water
  • Golden hour ride with mild rapids
  • Ends with riverside dinner and bonfire
  • Romantic, relaxed, and ridiculously photogenic

Jungle Explorer Run (Class II–III)

  • Nature meets adventure
  • Winding rivers through dense green canopies
  • Wildlife sightings and hidden waterfall stops
  • Medium-level excitement with max-level beauty

Extreme Expedition – 2-Day Overnight (Class III–V)

  • The full send package
  • Multi-day trip with camping
  • Includes meals, night hikes, and stargazing
  • For those who want to escape civilization (and maybe Wi-Fi)

Rapid Rookies Bootcamp

  • Learn-to-raft program for first-timers
  • Safety drills, basic paddling, mock rescue scenarios
  • Ends with a full river run to flex your new skills
  • Great for team-building and youth groups

Girls Go Wild (All-Female Adventure Trip)

  • Empowering, splashy, and sass-loaded
  • Led by expert female guides
  • A mix of thrill, wellness, and sisterhood
  • Includes riverside yoga and wine night

Corporate Flow – Team Rafting Retreat

  • Where team-building gets wet
  • Custom itineraries for companies
  • On-river leadership exercises + off-river workshops
  • “Trust falls” but make it Class III
